We understand the critical customer segments for IOCL having unique differentiated needs "beyond fuel"

IOCL can service the gaps through compelling value propositions

Fleet Owner

Values convenience & complete visibility of daily operations, requires economical resources & low-cost financing options to effectively manage their fleets.

Retail Customer

Values service & product quality, bike/drive-specific customers are price conscious, while busy motorists are more brand conscious.

RO Owner

Values convenience in daily operations & well integrated mutually profitable partnerships. Requires streamlined processes and efficient supply chain management.

MSME

Values economical but quality options, requires access to affordable and reliable fuel sources, along with flexible working capital solutions to support their business operations.

Farmer

Prioritizes the safety and security of their families and agricultural businesses. Requires dependable fuel supply for irrigation and farming equipment, along with support services that address their specific safety concerns.

Homemaker

Requires access to reliable cooking gas (LPG) supply and convenient refill options. They also appreciate value-added services such as home delivery and responsive customer support.
